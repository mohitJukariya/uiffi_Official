Co - Founder

Mr Udit Tyagi is the youngest son of Mr Sanjay Tyagi and Suman Tyagi, born in 1998 in a middle class family. Udit achieved his secondary and higher secondary education from St.John’s Senior Secondary School in Roorkee, obtaining a diploma in Electrical Engineering from Balwant Singh Mukhiya College of Polytechnic Roorkee with first division grades. He then went on to receive a Bachelor’s degree in Arts - English and Sociology - from Swami Vivekanand Subharti University with first division grades before completing a Post Graduation in Hindi Sahitya from Suresh Gyan Vihar University in Jaipur.
Udit began his theatre journey in 2015 with Sanskar Bharti or Rangmanch Theatre in Roorkee. Working with NSD Senior Teacher and renowned actor Uttara Bhaokar Mam, Udit gained knowledge of the right vision, thinking, and right path. He was then given the chance to work with NSD Teacher, performing several plays under their direction, and also worked as an actor with Kshitij Theatre.
During the Covid-19 pandemic, he worked with Sonu Sood to support those in need. Udit is also an avid physical activity enthusiast, having earned a Yellow Belt from Haridwar District Taekwondo Association after completing his training.
Currently, Udit is actively involved in Mumbai with acting and film festivals.
