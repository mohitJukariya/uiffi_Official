Workshop

Sanatani Tapobhoomi Society provides year-round opportunities to learn and grow as an artist. Our master classes, workshops, and short programs are designed, planned, and facilitated by art practitioners, teachers, and artists to help you reach your creative and personal goals. We specialize in a wide range of topics, including acting and theatre, clowning, voice and speech, voice-overs, physical theatre and contemporary movement, improvisation skills, writing, storytelling, and interdisciplinary approaches between literature and the visual arts. Through our series of workshops, we strive to create long-term learning experiences that emphasize storytelling and encourage creativity.

FILM FESTIVAL 2023

Uttarakhand International Film Festival of India  has featured works that center the actor. It focuses on films and plays, which allow actors to explore and create innovative expressions.
