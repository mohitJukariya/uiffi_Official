Uttarakhand International Film Festival of India 

Uttarakhand International Film Festival of India  has featured works that center the actor. It focuses on films and plays, which allow actors to explore and create innovative expressions.
The idea is to reach out across the globe and bring innovative work to the city of Mathura. The festival encourages any kind of theatrical presentation, such as a full-length play, short play, poetry presentation, story drama, solo performance, short-film, documentary film and any new creative innovation.
This festival will take place in the first year from 20 to 25 February 2023. The festival will take place entirely at a physical location. Eminent film and theater artists from across the country and abroad will participate in this festival.
For more details and information, get in touch with us on miffi.official@gmail.com or call us on +918652003802.
